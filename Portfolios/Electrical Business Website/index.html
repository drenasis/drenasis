Yes. We handle inspections, landlord checks and minor works certification where it is needed.

Do you work with businesses?

Yes. We help small businesses, offices, shops and landlords with repairs, maintenance and planned electrical work.

Can you quote before visiting?

Sometimes. For simple jobs we can often give guidance by phone. Larger or unclear jobs usually need a proper look first.
